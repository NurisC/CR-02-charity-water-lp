About Us

charity: water is a nonprofit organization on a mission to bring clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. Since 2006, charity: water has funded clean water projects for millions of people around the world, believing that access to clean water changes everything—health, education, and opportunity.

Our goal: End the water crisis in our lifetime by inspiring generosity and providing 100% transparency. Every dollar donated goes directly to water projects, and every project is tracked and reported back to donors.

Who we are: A passionate team, a global community of supporters, and local partners working together to create lasting change—one community at a time.

Why Water?

Nearly 1 in 10 people worldwide live without access to clean and safe drinking water. This crisis affects health, education, and livelihoods—especially for women and children who often spend hours each day collecting water.

Why does charity: water focus on water? Because clean water changes everything. It improves health, keeps kids in school, gives families time to earn an income, and helps communities thrive. When a community gets access to clean water, it can break the cycle of poverty and unlock opportunities for a better future.

By providing clean and safe water, charity: water is helping people live healthier, more productive lives and building a foundation for lasting change.
